Enchiladas

A Tray of Delicious Enchiladas

This is a tray of delicious looking enchiladas. Probably green enchiladas, but there could be a couple green enchiladas in there. Don't they just look good? Filled with broiled chicken and slow roasted Pork, they are delectable, and a Fan Favorite.

Ingredients

  • Broiled and Shredded Chicken
  • Slow Roasted Pork
  • Green and Red Enchilada Sauce
  • Plenty of Cheese
  • Rolled Tortillas

Steps to Make

  1. Cook Chicken or Pork
  2. Mix Sauce and Cheese
  3. Roll Sauce, Cheese, and Meat in tortilla and place in pan
  4. Bake on 400 degrees Farenheit, for 45 minutes in Oven
  5. Remove from Oven and let them cool
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
