Garlic Bread

This is THE recipe for a greatest food of all time, Garlic Bread.

This recipe describes the ingredients and the steps needed to construct a delicious garlic bread! Garlic Breads goes great with alot of things, but when it's delicious on it's own, the symphony of dinner sings!

Ingredients

  • Foccacia Bread
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Butter
  • Minced Garlic
  • Romano Cheese

Steps to Make!

  1. Cut Foccacia bread loaf in half.
  2. Melt butter and baste the top of the bread slices with it.
  3. Grate the cheese, and cover the top of the buttered bread slice with it.
  4. Sprinkle the minced garlic on top of the bread, and spread it evenly.
  5. Put in the oven for 10 minutes at 400 degrees!
  6. Enjoy!
